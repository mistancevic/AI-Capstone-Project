STAGE 1 · UNDERSTAND THE PLAN
Targets arrive as jargon — grams, kcal, ratios. Clients start the plan without truly understanding their numbers.
STAGE 2 · EAT & LOG DAILY
Logging every meal, all day, every day — the classic reason tracking apps get abandoned. One-tap confirmations in v1 absorb most of it.
STAGE 2 · HANDLE DISRUPTIONS
Decision paralysis when the day breaks: fuzzy options, no math done. The default becomes skipping the meal — protein missed, energy gone, guilt loop.
