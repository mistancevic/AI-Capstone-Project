The logging cliff — where trackers get abandoned (≈70–86% quit). Confirmed across three channels. v1’s one-tap confirmations absorb most of it.
STAGE 2 · HANDLE DISRUPTIONS−25 → −30
The modal reaction to a broken day isn’t a skipped meal — it’s the “what-the-hell” binge (one off-plan lunch → “day’s ruined” → overeat). A third adapt; a minority skip, often on purpose. PlateMate answers all three: the next plan-consistent bite, math shown, no shame. PLATEMATE V1 FOCUS Adjacent — Social & Travel (−12): the cleanest competitive whitespace.
