## Incumbent strengths (honest moat assessment)

- **MFP:** encyclopaedic database (20.5M foods), fast barcode flow, decade of
  saved meals = real switching costs; users complain and stay. Rated 4.0–4.4★
  across ~2.9M ratings — top complaints are monetization/UI, not journey.
- **MacroFactor:** adaptive weekly recalibration, praised through plateaus,
  and **explicitly guilt-free positioning** ("no red bars or shame
  notifications"). The closest incumbent philosophically.
- **Lose It!:** generous free tier. **Lifesum:** welcoming onboarding, ~65M users.

## Strategic corrections this forces

1. **Guilt-free is not white space — it is table stakes.** MacroFactor
   already owns guilt-free positioning. Our differentiator was never
   guilt-free alone: it is the in-the-moment decision against a
   coach-prescribed plan, with the coach escalation loop and safety tiers.
   Strategy notes amended accordingly.
2. **The client-side disruption need is latent; the coach-side version is
   felt.** No consumer complaint demand exists to ride — but coaches feel
   clients falling off between check-ins (their churn). The latent/felt
   split maps onto the two customers: sell the felt pain (coach), deliver
   against the latent one (client). Third independent argument for
   coach-led distribution.
3. **Pitch discipline:** never claim "incumbents fail at the journey"
   (they're rated 4+ stars); claim "incumbents don't attempt the moment we
   serve" — supported by the zero-complaints finding and MFP's blog-post-only
   answer to a broken day.
4. **Ethics framing, now citable:** abandonment isn't always negative and
   the category carries documented ED harm potential — our safety tiers,
   banned compensation language, and pause mode are the design response;
   say so on demo day.
5. **Investigation 4 must scrutinize MacroFactor hardest** (adaptive +
   guilt-free; gaps expected: no coach-plan ingestion, no broken-day
   recompute, no human escalation).
6. **Academic prior art to cite:** OnTrack and Slip Buddy prove the lapse
   moment matters and that no mainstream product translates the research —
   whitespace confirmation from the literature side.
