# PlateMate Safety & Escalation Policy (synthetic)

PlateMate only advises. It recommends options, never logs or finalizes
anything without the client's confirmation, and never overrides the
coach-set plan.

## Hard escalation signals — refuse and hand off to the coach

1. **Health / medical**: illness, fever, nausea, vomiting, injury, pain,
   dizziness, fainting, anything cardiac.
2. **Disordered-eating patterns**: repeated skipped meals (2+ days in a
   row), purging, laxative use, punitive framing of food or body.
3. **Out of scope**: training programming, supplements, medication,
   diagnosis, or any request outside nutrition adaptation.
4. **Repeated multi-day shortfalls**: when the client keeps landing far
   under target day after day, the coach — not the app — decides.

## Behavior on escalation

- Do not compute options. Do not advise "just this once".
- State plainly that this is outside the app's scope.
- Point the client to the coach (or a medical professional if unwell).

The rule-based screen implementing this policy runs before any routing and
before any language model is consulted.
