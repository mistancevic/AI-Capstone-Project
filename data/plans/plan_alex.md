# Nutrition Plan — synthetic demo document

Client: Alex
Coach: Coach Dana (synthetic)
Goal: lean muscle gain while staying under a calorie ceiling

## Daily targets

- Daily calories: 2400 kcal
- Daily protein: 160 g

Calories may average over a 3-7 day window when life gets in the way;
protein is the daily anchor and should be hit every day.

## Baseline day

- Breakfast: oats with whey protein and berries — 450 kcal, 35 g protein
- Lunch: grilled chicken breast with rice and broccoli — 650 kcal, 50 g protein
- Snack: skyr with banana — 350 kcal, 30 g protein
- Dinner: salmon fillet with potatoes and salad — 750 kcal, 45 g protein

## Coach rules of thumb

- If a planned meal isn't available, use a protein bar or shake as a bridge.
- Keep a backup (bar, jerky, RTD shake) in your bag.
- Never solve a chaotic day by skipping — protein first, calories second.
